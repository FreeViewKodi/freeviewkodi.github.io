Organized Content Management

The FreeViewKodi addon provides a clean and intuitive way to browse your content directly within the Kodi interface. By using a simple, structured JSON format, your TV shows, movies, and music videos are automatically organized into easy-to-navigate menus. This means less time searching and more time enjoying your media.

Local & Customizable Data

Unlike other add-ons that rely on external servers and APIs, FreeViewKodi sources all its content from local JSON files. This gives you complete control over your media library. You can add, remove, or modify content simply by editing a text file, making it a truly personalized experience.
